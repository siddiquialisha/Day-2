What I learned today : 
The skill of writing clear, specific instructions to get the best possible responses from an AI like Claude.
Weak vs Improved Prompt :
  Weak Prompt:
 "Write something about marketing."
    This gives Claude almost no direction — no audience, no goal, no format, no tone. 
    The output will be generic and likely unusable.

  Improved Prompt :
   "Write a 150-word Instagram caption for a small bakery launching a new mango cheesecake. 
   The tone should be warm, playful, and include a call-to-action encouraging followers to visit this weekend."

  
    This prompt specifies: platform, length, product, tone, and desired action — so Claude can deliver something immediately useful.

    Three practical benefits of writing better prompts:

Save time — Fewer back-and-forth revisions; get the right answer faster.
Better quality output — Specific inputs lead to specific, useful, high-quality results.
Unlock AI's full potential — Most people use only 10% of what Claude can do; good prompts reveal the rest.
